Help to interpret the results

This analysis let you compare the distribution of COGs for each COG category within genomes of interest. Each bar represent the number of COG identified.
Multiples occurences of a single COG definitions/genome are clustered as one. COGs definitions associated with multiple categories are counted as multiple separate entities.

- Click on bars to get a list of the corresponding loci.
- Click on labels to get venn diagram of the category (max 6 species will be diplayed).
