Help to interpret the results

The interactive Venn diagram let you visualize how many Kegg orthologs are shared among the selected genomes or a subset of genomes of interest, and how many are unique to each genome.
Additionally, the size of each list can be visualized in the barplot and finally, the number of unique or shared Kegg orthologs can be appreciated in the last plot.
Clicking on the reported numbers you will be redirected to the list of identified Ko entries and definitions.
